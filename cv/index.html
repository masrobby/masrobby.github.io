Publications

  • 2023.12.29
    Limits of water maser kinematics: insights from the high-mass protostar AFGL 5142-MM1
    Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society
    Multi-epoch VLBI observations measures 3D velocities of masers in MYSO outflows, assuming that they co-propagate with the shock. Our water maser data for a bowshock outflow in AFGL5142 depicts that they actually propagate slower than the bowshock. This underlines the importance of long-term monitoring of maser kinematics to understand the true nature of the outflows.
